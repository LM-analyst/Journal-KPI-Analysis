# Journal-KPI-Analysis
This repository is to showcase the work I undertook to analyse and communicate the performance of journals of interest.

# Business context
Multiple stakehodlers were independently reporting on journal KPIs using time-consuming manual methods, this led to inconsistencies in how metrics were reported and duplication of effort. In response, I created a Power BI dashboard to unify data from a variety of sources and create a single source of truth that could be utilised by a variety of stakeholders. This involved collecting data on submissions, publications, authors and editors for each journal from a variety of sources and building a complex data model to form relationships and extract insights.

# Business impact
The dashboard was updated on a monthly basis to provide an overview of journal performance and was used by a variety of stakeholders to inform strategic decisions, such as when to apply to journal indexes, how to increase author/editor diversity and how to drive submissions/publications.

# Tools used
- Excel
- Power BI desktop (Power Query, DAX, custom measures)
